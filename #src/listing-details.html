Investment Offering

SAB Capital is pleased to present Rite Aid in Wilmington, Delaware. This is a leasehold investment, with the tenant currently under a corporately guaranteed triple net lease (NNN) with zero landlord responsibilities. Its location on a signalized hard corner creates strong exposure on three separate thoroughfares and creates visibility from I-95 which sees over 84,000 vehicles per day. The site is part of the Adams Four Shopping Center, a 74,000 square foot retail center occupied largely by local tenants.

  • Rite Aid in Wilmington, DE | Largest and Most Populous City in Delaware

  • High Traffic Location | Visibility From I-95 (84,000+ Vehicles Per Day)
  • Signalized Hard Corner Location | Strong Exposure on West 3rd Street, West 4th Street, and North Adams Street
  • Well-Performing Location | Site Ranks 5th out of All DE Rite Aid Locations in Terms of Customer Visits
  • Dense Local Trade Area | Over 1.6M Square Feet of Existing Retail Space Within 1 Mile
  • Part of the Adams Four Shopping Center | 74,000+ Square Foot Retail Center Largely Occupied by Local Shops
