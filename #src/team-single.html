Preet Sabharwal

MANAGING PARTNER

Preet got his start in commercial real estate in 2007 at a leading national firm. As a leading commercial real estate broker, Preet saw an opportunity to lead his team in the most prosperous and ambitious direction they could have traveled. Today, Preet uses his more than a decade of experience in commercial real estate and over $3 billion in sales over his career span to provide strategic direction to grow SAB Capital across a nationwide approach.

A graduate of Hunter College, Preet holds a Bachelor’s degree in Science, Accounting, and Economics. Prior to co-founding SAB Capital, Preet served as Senior Vice President of Investments for a national commercial real estate brokerage firm. Preet’s real estate expertise includes single and multi-tenant assets. The breadth of his work spans retail, medical, and industrial portfolios; acquisition and disposition work for REITs; extensive advising on 1031 exchanges; and sale-leaseback advisory. He loves providing sound and straightforward advice to clients, and his ability to tell the honest truth and focused determination to help clients achieve their goals are why he loves his job.

Preet is a big believer in self-development and constantly challenges himself and his team members to achieve new grounds. Above and beyond the rewarding functions of being a founder of SAB Capital, Preet shares his life with his wife Tiffany and daughter Pia in their home in the Flatiron neighborhood of Manhattan.
