SAB 1031 PROCESS

Our carefully crafted process allows us to guide a diverse client base through the complexities of a 1031 exchange with the resources they need to see success.

  • 01

    Education

    SAB believes that an informed buyer is an empowered buyer. To help our clients navigate the 1031 process and make intelligent decisions, we provide in-depth education and expert insight about the net lease sector and the intricacies of exchange. During our education sessions, we will discuss tenant financials, different lease types, lease lengths, cap rates, and other key information.

  • 02

    Deal Team

    A successful exchange is a group effort. To help clients cover all their bases, we provide them with a team of trusted professionals including brokers, attorneys, title companies, and others who will help effectively manage the financial, legal, and logistical facets of an exchange.

  • 03

    Deal Sourcing and Presentation

    Finding the right property means partnering with the right broker. SAB’s team of experts maintains insider access to public and off-market deals from merchant developers, REITs, private equity firms, institutional and private investors across the country. We vet and investigate each new property and seller so that clients can make responsible offers and won’t miss out on opportunities.
