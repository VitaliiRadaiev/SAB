Mark Pomella is a Senior Investment Sales Associate specializing in the disposition and acquisition of net lease properties across the United States. With a focus on industrial, Mark takes pride in cultivating loyal relationships with industry-leading private clients, developers, and private equity firms to create targeted investment opportunities for his clients.

Mark received a Bachelor's Degree from Williams College. Prior to SAB Capital, Mark served as an investment sales agent at Marcus & Millichap. Mark takes pride in his ability to track and predict the market, allowing his clients to benefit from his insights. He enjoys building relationships and working on complex transactions with and for the clients he describes as kind, fun, and intelligent people.

The key to a productive day for Mark is preparation and waking up early to read and think. When he’s not in the office, Mark enjoys spending time outdoors and exercising.
