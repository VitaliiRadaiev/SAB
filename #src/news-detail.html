New York, NY

CoStar Group Inc., a leading provider of CRE analytics and online marketplaces, recently announced the Power Broker Quarterly Deal winners for the third quarter of 2021.

SAB Capital is pleased to announce that Managing Partner Zach Weiss, and Senior Investment Sales Associate Jordan Koeppel emerged as winners in the Greater Albany and Cleveland markets, respectively. Weiss closed a 14,550 SF Walgreens in Amsterdam, NY at a selling price of $6,052,000. Koeppel closed a 111,152 SF grocery-anchored shopping center in the heart of downtown Euclid, OH, at a selling price of $13,250,000.

Please help us congratulate Zach and Jordan on their CoStar Power Broker Quarterly Deals win.

For more information, visit: https://www.costarpowerbrokers.com/quarterly-deals/

SAB Capital is a national Real Estate Investment Sales firm dedicated to assisting clients in the acquisition and disposition of net-leased, industrial, and sale-leasebackproperties, as well as 1031 exchange advisory services. We provide brokerage expertise with the highest commitment and attention to detail, ensuring maximum yield and a faster turnaround time. Each client is advised on exit and entrance strategies aimed at achieving their investment goals.

CoStar Group, Inc. (NASDAQ: CSGP) is the leading provider of commercial real estate information, analytics and online marketplaces. Founded in 1987, CoStar conducts expansive, ongoing research to produce and maintain the largest and most comprehensive database of commercial real estate information. Our suite of online services enables clients to analyze, interpret and gain unmatched insight on commercial property values, market conditions and current availabilities. Through LoopNet, the Company operates the most heavily trafficked commercial real estate marketplace online with more than 9 million registered members. Apartments.com is a premier online apartment resource for renters that matches apartment seekers with great apartment homes and provides property managers and owners a proven platform for marketing their properties. CoStar operates websites that have over 19 million unique monthly visitors in aggregate during January 2015. Headquartered in Washington, DC, CoStar maintains offices throughout the U.S., in Europe and in Toronto, Canada with a staff of over 2,400 worldwide, including the industry’s largest professional research organization. For more information, visit www.costargroup.com.
